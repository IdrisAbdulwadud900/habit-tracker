Step 1 of 2

Who do you want to become?

Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become. Your habits shape your identity—and your identity shapes your habits.

I am

Examples: "someone who shows up every day" · "a person who finishes what they start" · "organized and in control"

Step 2 of 2

Audit your current reality

Awareness comes before desire. List everything you currently do in a day. Then score each habit based on whether it helps you become who you want to be.

The Habits Scorecard:
+ Good — Reinforces your desired identity
= Neutral — Neither helps nor hurts
- Bad — Works against who you want to become

Be honest. No one is judging. This audit is for your eyes only.
